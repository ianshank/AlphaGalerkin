# Business & Proposals

Non-engineering material — SBIR/STTR proposal development, commercialization, and
partner outreach — consolidated here from the previously scattered top-level
`proposal/`, `sbir/`, and `partners/` directories.

> The machine-readable SBIR **configs** (`config/proposals/*.yaml`) stay in
> `config/` because they are loaded by `scripts/run_sbir_*.py`; this hub holds the
> **prose**.
