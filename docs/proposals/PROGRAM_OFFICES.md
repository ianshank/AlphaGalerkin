## Engagement Best Practices

### Before Submission
1. **Read the full solicitation** including evaluation criteria and scoring rubric
2. **Contact the TPOC** (Technical Point of Contact) listed in the solicitation
3. **Ask**: "Would a proposal addressing [specific capability] be responsive to this topic?"
4. **Attend pre-proposal conferences** when offered (especially for DoD topics)
5. **Review prior awards** on SBIR.gov for the same topic/office

### TPOC Engagement Template
> Subject: Inquiry re: [Solicitation ID] - AI-Guided Adaptive Mesh Refinement
>
> Dear [TPOC Name],
>
> I am writing regarding [solicitation ID]. Our technology, AlphaGalerkin, uses
> Monte Carlo Tree Search to guide Galerkin finite element mesh refinement,
> achieving [specific benchmark result]. This addresses your stated need for
> "[quote from solicitation]."
>
> Would a proposal focused on [specific capability] be responsive to this topic?
> I would welcome the opportunity to discuss alignment in more detail.
