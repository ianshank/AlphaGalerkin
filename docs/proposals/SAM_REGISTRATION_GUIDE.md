# SAM.gov Registration Guide for SBIR/STTR Submissions

## Overview
All SBIR/STTR applicants must be registered in SAM.gov before submitting proposals. Registration is free but takes 3-4 weeks end-to-end. Start immediately.

## Step-by-Step Checklist

### Step 1: Obtain an EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- **Source**: IRS Form SS-4 (online at irs.gov)
- **Timeline**: Instant (online) or 4 weeks (mail)
- **Cost**: $0
- **Notes**: Required even for single-member LLCs. The EIN is the business tax ID.

### Step 2: Obtain a UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)
- **Source**: Assigned automatically during SAM.gov registration
- **Timeline**: Instant upon SAM.gov entity validation
- **Cost**: $0
- **Notes**: Replaces the legacy DUNS number. Do NOT pay any third party for a UEI.

### Step 3: Register Entity in SAM.gov
- **URL**: https://sam.gov
- **Timeline**: 7-10 business days (active registration), up to 25 days if IRS validation delayed
- **Cost**: $0
- **Required Information**:
  - Legal business name and DBA (if applicable)
  - EIN (from Step 1)
  - Physical address (not PO Box)
  - Bank account routing number and account number (for EFT payments)
  - NAICS codes (see below)
  - Business type: "Small Business" + applicable socioeconomic categories
  - POC information (name, email, phone)

### Step 4: NAICS Codes
Select the following NAICS codes during SAM.gov registration:

| Code | Description | Use For |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **541715** | Research and Development in the Physical, Engineering, and Life Sciences (except Nanotechnology and Biotechnology) | Primary code for all SBIR proposals |
| **541511** | Custom Computer Programming Services | Secondary code for software-focused proposals |
| **541512** | Computer Systems Design Services | Tertiary (if applicable) |

### Step 5: CAGE Code Assignment
- **Source**: Assigned automatically by the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) during SAM.gov validation
- **Timeline**: 2-3 weeks after SAM registration completes
- **Cost**: $0
- **Notes**: Required for DoD proposals. Non-DoD agencies (NSF, DOE) do not require CAGE.

### Step 6: SBIR.gov Company Registration
- **URL**: https://www.sbir.gov/registration
- **Timeline**: 1-2 business days
- **Cost**: $0
- **Prerequisites**: Active SAM.gov registration + UEI
- **Notes**: Required for proposal submission through agency portals

### Step 7: SBA Company Registry
- **URL**: https://www.sbir.gov/sbirsearch/firm/all
- **Timeline**: Automatic after SBIR.gov registration
- **Notes**: Publicly searchable directory of SBIR-registered firms

## SBIR Eligibility Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Business Structure** | For-profit small business (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp) |
| **Size** | <500 employees (NAICS 541715) |
| **Ownership** | >50% owned by U.S. citizens or permanent residents |
| **PI Employment** | PI must be "primarily employed" (>50% effort) by the small business |
| **Location** | Primary place of performance in the United States |
| **R&D Work** | Minimum 2/3 of Phase I work performed by the small business |

## Registration Timeline

| Action | Timeline | Status |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Apply for EIN (IRS) | Day 1 | Instant online |
| Start SAM.gov registration | Day 1 | Begin immediately |
| UEI assigned | Day 1-3 | Automatic during SAM |
| SAM.gov active | Day 7-25 | Wait for validation |
| CAGE code assigned | Day 14-35 | Automatic (DoD only) |
| SBIR.gov registration | Day 8-26 | After SAM active |
| Ready to submit proposals | Day 14-35 | All systems active |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. **Do NOT pay for a UEI** - it is free from SAM.gov
2. **Do NOT use a PO Box** as your business address
3. **Keep SAM.gov registration active** - it expires annually, renew 60 days before expiration
4. **Match legal name exactly** across EIN, SAM.gov, and proposal documents
5. **Register before solicitations open** - the 3-4 week timeline cannot be compressed
